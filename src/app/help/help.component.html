How to use voice search?

You can use your voice to do searches by simply tapping on the microphone on the search bar and speaking your query.

How to search images on Susper?

You can search Susper for images, just like you can search for websites. For example, you can check out pictures of potential vacation destinations, or find an image to use in an upcoming presentation.

  1. Go to susper.com
  2. Search your query
  3. Then select the Image tab, located below the search bar

How to block explicit content on Susper?

You can filter explicit search results on Susper, like pornography, with the SafeSearch setting. SafeSearch isn’t 100% accurate. But it can help you avoid explicit and inappropriate search results on your phone, tablet, or computer.

  1. Go to Search Settings
  2. Under "SafeSearch filters," check or uncheck the box next to "Turn on SafeSearch."
  3. At the bottom of the page, select Save.

How to report a problem with Susper?

If you're having a problem with Susper Search, or want to tell us your thoughts about how it's working, you can send a feedback report to Susper.

  1. Open our Contact page.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  3. Click on Contact Form
  4. Fill out the information
  5. Click Send

Images aren't loading

If images aren't loading, please try one of the follow

  1. Try private browsing
  2. Check the speed of your internet connection
  3. Clear your cache and cookies
  4. Turn off any toolbars and extensions
  5. Turn on JavaScript

How to search on Susper?

Using Susper is pretty easy. Let's start with some basics

  1. On the main page of Susper, enter your queries. Susper will process them and the result will be displayed on the screen
  2. You can also use voice search by clicking on the mic in the search bar.
  3. When the search result has been displayed, click on Images to search for images
