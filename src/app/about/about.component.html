About Susper

Susper is a decentral Search Engine that uses the peer to peer system 'YaCy' and 'Apache Solr' to crawl and index search results.

About YaCy

YaCy is a free search engine that anyone can use to build a search portal for their intranet or to help search the public internet. Read more about YaCy here - YaCy Decentralized Web Search
